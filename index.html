SPM1: Figure 1 of the Summary for Policymakers in the IPCC's Special Report on the Global Warming of 1.5℃

The IPCC Special Report on 1.5℃, released in December 2018, presents the most up-to-date research on the science around the most ambitious goal of the Paris Agreement: to hold the global average temperature anomaly to below 1.5℃ above pre-industrial levels.

Here we present an interactive version of figure 1 in the Summary for Policymakers document. Figure 1 acts as a summary for the material discussed in Chapter 1 of the main report - including key definitions of terms such as global warming, the pre-industrial period, and the human contribution to warming to date. We present an interactive version of the figure so users can understand how decisions they make at a national and global policy level impact the likely outcomes of us remaining below 1.5℃.
